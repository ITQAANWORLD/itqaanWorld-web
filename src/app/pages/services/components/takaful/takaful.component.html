Takaful Motor:

What Cover do We have?

  • Your policy cover will normally run for 12 months and is renewable annually.
  • Takaful will cover you for any loss or damage to the insured vehicle and its spare part accessories.
  • Takaful will cover you for any amount that you as the owner of the motor vehicle may be legally liable for
  • Takaful will cover you in respect of death of or bodily injury to any person including all passengers in the vehicles except you, your spouse, your children or the driver.
  • Takaful will cover you in respect of property damage except those owned by you or the driver at the time of accident or property that has been entrusted to you or is in your custody or control.

How much must I pay if I have a claim?

  • Accidental damage: 2.5% of Sum Insured, Minimum Kshs 15,000/-, Maximum Kshs 100,000/-
  • Partial Theft of vehicle parts: 2.5% of Sum Insured, Minimum Kshs 15,000/-
  • Theft where vehicle has anti theft device: 10% of Sum Insured, Minimum Kshs 20,000/-
  • Theft for vehicle without anti theft device: 20% of Sum Insured, Minimum Kshs 20,000/-
  • Theft for vehicle with tracking device: 2.5% of Sum Insured, Minimum Kshs 20,000/-
  • Third Party Property Damage: Kshs 7,500/-
  • Young drivers below 21 years: Ksh.5,000 Additional
  • Drivers with less than 1 year driving experience: Kshs 5,000 Additional

For Total Loss & Theft of vehicle we will also ask for the following:

  • Duplicate certificate of Insurance
  • Valuation/Assessment report
  • Original logbook in our insured’s name
  • Copy of PIN Certificate
  • Copy of National Identity card
  • For companies – Certificate of registration
  • Signed but undated transfer form
  • Spare car keys
  • Spare wheel, jack & spanner
  • For total loss surrender the salvage

Motorcycle Comprehensive:

What Cover do We have?

  • Your policy cover will normally run for 12 months and is renewable annually.
  • Takaful will cover you for any loss or damage to the insured vehicle and its spare part accessories.
  • Takaful will cover you for any amount that you as the owner of the motor vehicle may be legally liable for
  • Takaful will cover you in respect of death of or bodily injury to any person including all passengers in the vehicles except you, your spouse, your children or the driver.
  • Takaful will cover you in respect of property damage except those owned by you or the driver at the time of accident or property that has been entrusted to you or is in your custody or control.

How much must I pay if I have a claim?

  • Accidental Damage = 5% of sum Insured, Minimum Ksh.7,500
  • Theft with chain & padlock = 10% of Sum insured, Minimum Kshs 10,000/-
  • Theft without chain & Padlock = 20% of Sum insured, Minimum kshs 15,000/-
  • Third Party Property Damage = Kshs 3,000/-
  • Young Rider below 21 years = Ksh.2,000 Additional
  • Third Party Property Damage: Kshs 7,500/-
  • Young drivers below 21 years: Ksh.5,000 Additional

How do I make a claim?

You should report to us immediately. We will ask you for the following documents:

  • Completed Claim Form
  • Copy of the log book
  • Police Abstract Report
  • Motor Vehicle Inspection report (for third-party claims)
  • Copy of the Driver’s Driving licence
  • Notice of intention to prosecute(if any)

For Total Loss & Theft of vehicle we will also ask for the following:

  • Duplicate certificate of Insurance
  • Valuation/Assessment report
  • Original logbook in our insured’s name
  • Copy of PIN Certificate
  • Copy of National Identity card
  • For companies – Certificate of registration
  • Signed but undated transfer form
  • Spare car keys
  • Spare wheel, jack & spanner
  • For total loss surrender the salvage
